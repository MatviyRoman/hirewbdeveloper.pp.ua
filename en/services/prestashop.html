Frequently Asked

Can I migrate my store from another platform?

⌄
Yes, I help migrate from WooCommerce, OpenCart, Shopify and other platforms. We transfer everything: products, categories, orders, customers, ratings, and reviews. Nothing is lost, and SEO is preserved with proper redirects.

What payment systems can be integrated?

⌄
Stripe, Fondy, LiqPay, PayPal, 2Checkout, Skrill, Wise and others. All standard payment gateways are easy to integrate. Even non-standard services can be connected for specific needs.

How much does a custom theme cost?

⌄
Starting at $600 for a simple theme with basic design. For complex multi-store systems with payment integrations: $1500-3000+. Final pricing depends on functionality and complexity.

How is store security ensured?

⌄
SSL certificates, customer data encryption, XSS and CSRF protection, updated dependencies, regular PrestaShop updates, automated backups, and security scanning scripts.
